Multi Copy – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Multi Copy is a Chrome extension that helps users copy multiple items and paste them in order. We respect your privacy and do not collect, store, or sell personal data.

What data we collect

None. Multi Copy does not collect any personal information or usage data.

How your data is handled

  • Clipboard history and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage.
  • No data is transmitted to any server.
  • No analytics or tracking is used.

Permissions explained

  • Host access (all sites): Required to capture copy/paste events on any website.
  • Storage: Used to save your clipboard history locally.
  • Clipboard access: Required to read and paste your copied text.

Third-party services

None. Multi Copy does not use any third-party services.
