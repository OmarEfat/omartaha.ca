About

Multi Copy is a Chrome extension that makes multi-item clipboard workflows fast and reliable across sites and tabs. Copy multiple items and paste them in order, reorder your clipboard history with drag-and-drop, and use quick enable/reset controls in a clean popup UI.

Privacy Policy

We do not collect any data.

  • Clipboard history is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to any server
  • No analytics or tracking is used

Permissions Explained

  • Host access (all sites): Required to capture copy/paste events on any website
  • Storage: Used to save your clipboard history locally
  • Clipboard access: Required to read and paste your copied text
