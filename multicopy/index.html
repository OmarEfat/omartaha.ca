Features

Copy multiple items to your clipboard and paste them in the exact order you need.

Privacy & Terms

Privacy Policy

We do not collect any data. Your privacy matters.

  • Clipboard history is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to any server
  • No analytics or tracking is used

Permissions Explained

  • Host access — Required to capture copy/paste events on the websites you visit.
  • Storage — Used to save your clipboard queue locally on your browser.
  • Clipboard access — Required to read the text you copy and inject it when you paste.
